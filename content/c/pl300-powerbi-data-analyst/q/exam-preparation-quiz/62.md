    - id: "answer2"
      title: "Scatter plot"
      explain: "Scatter plots are for high-density data, not for key metrics."
    - id: "answer3"
      title: "Treemap"
      explain: "Treemaps show hierarchical data, not single data points."
    - id: "answer4"
      title: "Ribbon chart"
      explain: "Ribbon charts are for displaying rank change, not key metrics."
